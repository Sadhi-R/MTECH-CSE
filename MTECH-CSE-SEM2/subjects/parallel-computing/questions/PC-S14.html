Question

List necessary conditions for deadlock.

DefinitionMutual exclusion, hold and wait, no preemption, circular wait.

Key Points

  • All four needed
  • Breaking any one prevents deadlock
  • Applies to locks and resources

Real-Time Example

Two threads each waiting for other's lock.
