Question

What are ghost nodes in distributed graphs?

DefinitionGhost nodes are replicated boundary vertices from neighboring partitions.

Key Points

  • Reduce remote access frequency
  • Need periodic synchronization
  • Support local computation

Real-Time Example

Shared border intersections between city zones.
