Question

Define strong scaling.

DefinitionStrong scaling studies speedup with fixed total problem size.

Key Points

  • Measures parallel efficiency under fixed work
  • Often limited by serial fraction
  • Related to Amdahl law

Real-Time Example

Same dataset processed using more processors.
