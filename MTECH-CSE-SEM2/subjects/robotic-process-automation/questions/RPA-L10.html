RPA ROI and Process Selection

High WeightageMid-II Q390%Unit-5Long
Question

How do you select right process for RPA and calculate ROI?

DefinitionRPA ROI compares automation benefits (time/cost/error reduction) against implementation and maintenance cost.

Introduction

Not every process is automation-ready.

Why It Is Needed

Right process selection determines success of RPA program.

Working Principle

Score process on volume, rule stability, digital inputs, exception rate.

Step-by-Step Explanation

  • Create selection matrix
  • Estimate baseline effort
  • Estimate automated effort
  • Compute payback period

Architecture / Diagram

ROI equation with annual savings and bot cost.

Algorithm

Process prioritization and ROI ranking algorithm.

Flowchart

Collect metrics -> score candidates -> estimate ROI -> prioritize rollout.

Real-Time Example

Automating monthly invoice entry gives immediate hours saved.

Advantages

  • Business-aligned automation
  • Improved stakeholder buy-in

Disadvantages

  • ROI can be overestimated if baseline data poor

Applications

  • Finance SSC
  • Insurance claims
  • HR operations

Exam Tips

Include both tangible and intangible benefits.

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring maintenance and license cost in ROI.

Difference Table

High-volume stable processes usually outperform complex exception-heavy ones.

Time Complexity

Computation O(number of candidate processes).

Space Complexity

Need historical transaction data and cost sheets.

Interview Questions

  • Management interviews often focus on ROI reasoning.

Memory Tricks

Automate high-volume stable tasks first for fastest payback.

5-Mark Answer

Define selection criteria and ROI formula.

10-Mark Answer

Present process scoring, estimation, and prioritization framework.
