ROI Formula Basics

Question Bank80%Unit-5Short
Question

Write simple RPA ROI expression.

DefinitionROI percentage equals (annual benefit - annual cost) divided by annual cost times 100.

Key Points

  • Needs accurate baseline
  • Include maintenance and license
  • Track post-go-live

Real-Time Example

If benefit 10 lakh and cost 5 lakh, ROI is 100%.
