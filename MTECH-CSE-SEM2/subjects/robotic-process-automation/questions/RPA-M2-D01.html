Q1: Audit Log relevance

High WeightageMid-II 2024-25100%Unit-3Short
Question

Explain the Audit Log and its relevance in automation control.

DefinitionAudit Log in Automation Anywhere Control Room records every bot action, user activity, and system event with timestamp and user identity.

Visual Example

Key Points

  • Tracks who deployed, scheduled, modified, or ran each bot.
  • Records login/logout, credential access, task success/failure.
  • Relevance: compliance (SOX, GDPR), security forensics, troubleshooting failed runs.
  • Enterprise governance — proves automation was controlled and traceable.

Real-Time Example

Bank audit: regulator asks who ran loan-processing bot on March 5 — Audit Log shows user, time, bot, result.

Exam Keyword Audit Log · Compliance · Traceability · Control Room

5-Mark Answer (Exam Ready)

Define Audit Log. What it records. Why needed for compliance and debugging. Bank example.

One-Line RevisionAudit Log = timestamped record of all bot/user actions — essential for compliance and debugging.
