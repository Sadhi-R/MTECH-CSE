Stable vs Unstable Sorting

Question

Differentiate stable and unstable sorting.

DefinitionStable sorting keeps equal keys in original relative order.

Key Points

  • Merge sort is stable
  • Quick sort is typically unstable
  • Stability matters with secondary keys
