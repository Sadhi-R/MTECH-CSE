Modular Inverse

Question Bank86%Unit-5Short
Question

What is modular inverse?

DefinitionInverse of a mod m is x such that ax congruent 1 mod m.

Key Points

  • Exists when gcd(a,m)=1
  • Computed by extended Euclid
  • Used in CRT

Real-Time Example

Inverse of 3 mod 11 is 4.

Exam Keyword EXTENDED EUCLID
